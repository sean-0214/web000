Project Overview

The International Quant Championship 2024, organized by WorldQuant, was a global competition that attracted over 4,300 teams from around the world. Our team successfully advanced to the global semi-final, achieving a ranking in the top 10.08% of all participating teams.

Technical Approach

Our approach focused on three main alpha strategies, each targeting different aspects of market behavior:

1. Fundamental Analysis Strategy

Developed a quantitative model that analyzed fundamental financial metrics to identify undervalued securities. This strategy incorporated earnings ratios, book values, and growth metrics to generate trading signals.

2. Volatility-Based Strategy

Created a volatility prediction model that identified opportunities in options markets and volatility arbitrage. The strategy used historical volatility patterns and implied volatility surfaces to generate alpha.

3. Volume-Momentum Strategy

Implemented a strategy that combined trading volume analysis with momentum indicators to identify short-term price movements. This approach proved particularly effective in capturing market microstructure inefficiencies.
